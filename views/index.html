Noodle's Notes

50 years of sailing in the Hawaiian islands

Download Blog

Bill "Noodle" Leary has spent the last 50 years cruising and racing throughout the Hawaiian islands. Wherever there's a safe, beautiful anchorage, you can be sure he's been there. He's done extensive research and taken meticulous notes that he's compiled into this free cruising guide to Hawaii, in the hopes that more sailors can enjoy the spectacular spots he's come to know and love.

Noodle continues to cruise around the world and is a devoted blogger on these trips. You can follow along on his ongoing adventures via the "Blog" link above.
